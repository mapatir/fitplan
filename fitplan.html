SUNDAY · ~90 MINUTES
Weekly prep
Covers Mon–Thu. Quick refresh Friday.
1
Proteins (40 min): Bake 3 lbs chicken breast at 400°F for 22–25 min. Brown 1 lb ground turkey on the stovetop simultaneously. Cool, slice, store separately.
2
Carbs (20 min): Cook 2 cups dry brown rice — makes ~6 cups cooked. Portion into containers: ½ cup each (fat loss) or ¾ cup (build phase).
3
Veggies (30 min): Two sheet pans — broccoli + zucchini + peppers. 425°F, 20–25 min. Olive oil spray, garlic powder, salt.
4
Assemble: 4 lunch containers (chicken + rice + greens) + 4 dinner containers (protein + veg ± sweet potato). Label by day.
5
Quick snacks: Hard-boil 6 eggs. Pre-portion ¾ cup Greek yogurt into small containers. Done.
6
Friday refresh (20 min): Quick cook — fresh protein for the weekend. This kills the urge to order out.
